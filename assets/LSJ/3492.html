ναύτ-ης , ου, ὁ, (ναῦς)
A.seaman, sailor, Il.19.375, Sapph.Supp.9.8, Pi.P.4.188, Pl.Plt.302a, etc.: as Adj., “ν. ὅμιλος” E.Hec.921 (lyr.); by sea, opp. πεζός (by land), A.Pers.719 (troch.).
II. passenger by sea, “ναύτην ἄγειν τινά” S.Ph.901: metaph., συμποσίου ναῦται mates in the drinking bout, Dionys.Eleg.5.
