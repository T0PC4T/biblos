ἀλαζ-ών [α^λ], όνος, ὁ, ἡ, (ἄλη) prop.
A.wanderer about country, vagrant, Alc. Com.31.
II. charlatan, quack, esp. of Sophists, Cratin.380, Ar.Nu.102, Pl.Chrm.173c, al.
2. braggart, boaster, X.Cyr.2.2.12, Arist.EN1127a21; title of play by Men.
3. Adj., boastful, pretentious, Hdt.6.12; “ἀ. λόγοι” Pl.R.560c: Comp. “-έστερος” Suid. s.v. εἴρων: Sup., ἡδονὴ ἀλαζονίστατον most shameless, Pl. Phlb.65c. Adv. Sup. “-έστατα, δρω-ν” Ael.NA4.29.
