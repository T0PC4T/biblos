κοιτών , ῶνος, ὁ,
A.bed-chamber, Ar.Fr.6, PTeb.120.14 (i B.C.), D.S.11.69, etc.; “ὁ ἐπὶ τοῦ κοιτῶνος” chamberlain, Act.Ap.12.20, Arr.Epict.3.22.15; ἐπὶ κ. Σεβαστοῦ, = Lat. cubicularius Augusti, CIG2947 (Caria, ii A.D.), cf. IG14.2143, al.: rejected by the Atticists, who hold δωμάτιον to be correct, cf. Poll.1.79, Phryn.227.
2. grave, IG14.464 (Catana).
3. nursery, ἐν κ. εἶναι to be an infant, minor, Just. Nov.155Praef.
II. landing-place, Stad.128.
