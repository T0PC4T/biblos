ἀποστομ-α^τίζω , (στόμα)
A.teach by word of mouth, teach by dictation, “γράμματα ἀ.” Pl.Euthd.277a: abs., ὅταν ἀ. ὑμῖν ὁ γραμματεύς ib.276c: —Pass., τὸ ἀποστοματιζόμενον dictated lesson, ibid., Arist.SE165b32.
2. interrogate, catechize, as a master his pupil, Ev.Luc.11.53, cf. Pl. ap. Poll.1.102（Pass.).
II. repeat by heart, Ath.8.359d, Antyll. ap.Orib.6.9.4: generally, recite, repeat, Plu.Thes.24.
